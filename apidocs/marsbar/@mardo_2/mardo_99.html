mardo_99

PURPOSE

method to convert SPM2 design to SPM99 design

SYNOPSIS

function o = mardo_99(o)

DESCRIPTION 

 method to convert SPM2 design to SPM99 design
 
 The conversion is crude, and only transfers those fields
 known to be of use in MarsBaR estimation
